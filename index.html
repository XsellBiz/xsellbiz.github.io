FAQ

Do you store our data?

By default, no. Solutions are typically designed to run in your environment. If a hosted component is required, we align on security, access, and retention upfront.

What systems can you work with?

Any source that can export data (CSV/Excel) or provide database/API access. KNIME workflows can connect to common databases and tools, and Excel remains the front-end where it makes sense.

What’s a typical timeline?

Most engagements start with a short assessment, followed by a focused build phase. Timelines depend on data sources and complexity, but we aim for practical delivery and quick wins.

How do you ensure KPI reliability?

We implement validation rules, consistency checks, and clear metric definitions so finance leadership can trust the numbers presented to management.
